Research Interests

My work focuses on security, privacy, and resilience in mobile networks, wearable systems, and privacy-preserving data sharing.

📡

Mobile Network Security

Securing 4G/5G and next-generation mobile networks against privacy and security threats.

🔐

Differential Privacy

Designing privacy-preserving algorithms and architectures for sensitive data publishing.

⌚

Wearable Data Sharing

Building secure and privacy-aware mechanisms for sharing physiological and medical data.

🛡️

Data Privacy

Protecting continuously generated personal and system data in connected environments.

🤖

Privacy in AI Systems

Studying privacy risks and utility trade-offs in AI-enabled and machine learning systems.
